How to Buy

Create a Wallet

Download MetaMask or your wallet of choice from the app store or Google Play for free. Desktop users, download the Google Chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

Get Some ETH

Have ETH in your wallet to switch to $PEPE. Buy ETH directly on MetaMask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on an exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to Uniswap

Connect to Uniswap by going to app.uniswap.org in Google Chrome or within the MetaMask browser. Connect your wallet, paste the $PEPE token address, select Pepe, and confirm. Sign when MetaMask prompts you.

Switch ETH for $BABYPEPE

Switch ETH for $BABYPEPE. With ZERO taxes, there’s no need for specific slippage, though it may be required during high volatility.
